About Us
Our Misson

We seek to maximise the realisation of
the innate potential of every African
child by combining gaming technology with tutelage
& providing easy access
(or increased exposure) to high-end technological resources.
This allows us to teach the child various
things and concepts whilst they are
having fun.

What we offer

Fun & Learning

# Online

  • Video games- educational and general
  • Lesson based Story books and videos

# Offline (At our gaming centres)

  • Video games- educational and general
  • Packages for life skills acquisition
  • Tech exposure

Fun Facts

Did you know?

  • kids learn better when they are having
    fun?
  • In the 21st century, kids have the most

What if we could teach them ‘things’
whilst they were having fun?
At A4k, we do just that!
